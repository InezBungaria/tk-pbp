About Us

What is Scrap.py?

Cleaning the environment,
one scrap at a time

Scrap.py is a waste-bank that was created to facilitate the community in managing waste in a sustainable manner. Scrap.py exists as our responsibility to the environment through 3R (reduce, reuse, recycle) activities.

Vision and Mission

Our Vision

Providing ethical, independent, and responsible Waste Management Solutions to our community

Our Mission

Empowering Waste Segregation

Increasing Community Participation in Environmental Management

Creating a More Creative, Innovative, Inspirational and Independent Society in the Field of Environmental Management
